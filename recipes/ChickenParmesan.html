Chicken Parmesan

Recipe

Chicken Parmesan, also known as Chicken Parmigiana, is a beloved Italian-American dish that features breaded chicken breast cutlets topped with marinara sauce and melted cheese, typically mozzarella and Parmesan. The preparation begins by coating the chicken breasts in flour, then dipping them in beaten eggs, and finally covering them with seasoned breadcrumbs. The breaded chicken is then pan-fried until golden and crispy. After frying, the chicken cutlets are placed in a baking dish, smothered in marinara sauce, and generously topped with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

The dish is then baked until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown. This process ensures that the chicken remains moist on the inside while achieving a crispy exterior. Chicken Parmesan is often served with pasta, making it a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family dinners. The combination of crispy chicken, savory marinara sauce, and melted cheese creates a dish that is rich in flavor and satisfying to eat

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts - 2 large, boneless and skinless
  • Salt and Pepper - to taste
  • Flour - 1/2 cup
  • Eggs - 2 large, beaten
  • Breadcrumbs - 1 cup (preferably Italian seasoned)
  • Parmesan Cheese - 1/2 cup, grated
  • Olive Oil - for frying
  • Marinara Sauce - 1 cup
  • Mozzarella Cheese - 1 cup, shredded
  • Fresh Basil or Parsley - for garnish

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingrediants
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Slice 2 large, boneless, skinless chicken breasts in half horizontally to make 4 cutlets. Season them with salt and pepper on both sides.
    • Set up three shallow dishes: one with 1/2 cup of flour, one with 2 beaten eggs, and one with 1 cup of Italian seasoned breadcrumbs mixed with 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  2. Coat the Chicken
    • Dredge each chicken cutlet in flour, shaking off excess.
    • Dip in beaten eggs, allowing excess to drip off.
    • Coat in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing lightly to adhere. Set aside.
  3. Fry the Chicken
    • Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add enough olive oil to coat the bottom.
    • Fry the chicken cutlets in batches for 3-4 minutes per side, until golden brown and cooked through. Transfer to a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  4. Assemble the Dish:
    • pread 1 cup of marinara sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
    • Arrange the fried chicken cutlets on top of the sauce.
    • Spoon additional marinara sauce over each cutlet.
    • Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the cutlets.
  5. Bake the Chicken
    • Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
    • Garnish with fresh basil or parsley before serving
